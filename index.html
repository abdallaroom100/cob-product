Corn-free popcorn?

Our snacks were inspired by people living with corn allergies, but we then realized that the majority of GMOs, additives, fillers, preservatives, fortification, and artificial ingredients & flavors were actually derived from corn. So we eliminated ALL of these from our corn-free snacks.

Our “popcorn”, made from ancient supergrain sorghum, is:

Easier to digest

Toddler Friendly

Sensitive gum safe (won’t get stuck in your teeth)

Pesticide free

3x protein, 5x iron, & 3x fiber (than popcorn per cup)
